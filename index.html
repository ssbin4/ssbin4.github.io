Sungbin Shin

Hi, I am a second-year Ph.D student in computer science and engineering at POSTECH, advised by Prof. Namhoon Lee.

My vision is to make machine learning models more accessible to everyone. As of now, I am interested in building efficient models through compression and understanding generalization of large-scale neural networks.

Contact me via ssbin4@postech.ac.kr if you have any questions.
